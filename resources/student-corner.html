Interview Guidance for Civil Engineers

1. Technical Mastery

Brush up on core subjects like Strength of Materials, Structural Analysis, Geotechnical Engineering, and Concrete Technology. Be prepared to answer questions about site safety and building codes.

2. The STAR Method (Behavioral)

Use Situation, Task, Action, Result to structure your answers. When asked about conflict or teamwork, clearly define the problem and the specific action
