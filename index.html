// About Civiline

Built for self-study, kept simple

CIVILINE brings together the scattered corners of civil engineering learning — journals, textbooks, software guides, and branch-specific notes — into one directory that's organized the way students and practicing engineers actually search: by branch, by topic, and by what they need to do next.

Video lectures are sourced from NPTEL, one of India's most trusted platforms for engineering education, and every written resource on this site is free to access.

  • 01 Organized by branch, not just alphabetically
  • 02 No paywalls, no sign-up required
  • 03 Updated as new NPTEL lectures and papers are released
  • 04 Built by and for self-taught engineers
